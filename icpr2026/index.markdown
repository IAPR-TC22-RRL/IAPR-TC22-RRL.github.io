For the first time in ICPR 2026, the IAPR [Technical Committee 22 (TC-22)](https://iapr-tc22.org/) proposes to acknowledge with a **Reproducible Research Label** badge those papers that have been accepted to the main conference and that, after a specific review of the associated code, are considered eligible to be labeled as [_reproducible_](https://www.acm.org/publications/policies/artifact-review-and-badging-current).

## Overview of the submission and review process

The review process is the following:

1. Each **author**{: .author} of an accepted paper who has checked the _RRPR badge_ option when submitting the paper to the main conference needs to carefully read the instructions for authors and editors, and confirm their participation.
2. The **ICPR Reproducible Research Chairs**{: .chairs} use the same system as for regular papers review ([CMT](https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICPR2026/)) to **assign reviewers to each created issue**{: .chairs}.
3. Each reviewer will check the code, write a short report with a recommendation for the meta-reviewers with [CMT](https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICPR2026/).
4. Eventually, the ICPR Reproducibility Chairs act as meta-reviewers and **decide on the RRPR badge attribution**{: .chairs}.

**The list of labeled papers** will appear in a section of the **ICPR official proceedings** dedicated to the label, as well as on the websites of **ICPR 2026** and the **IAPR Technical Committee 22** (TC-22).

You have detailed instructions in the corresponding pages for [authors]({{ "icpr2026/authors" | relative_url }}) and [reviewers]({{ "icpr2026/reviewers/" | relative_url }}).

## Deadlines

- Author's participation confirmation: 12/06
- All issues assigned to reviewers: 16/06
- Reviews deadline: 8/07
- Notification to authors: 10/08
