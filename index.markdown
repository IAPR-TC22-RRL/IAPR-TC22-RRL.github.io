For the first time, the ICPR committee proposes to deliver a **Reproducible Reasearch Label** to papers that have been accepted for the main conference.
This label is managed by the **ICPR Reproducible Research Chairs**{: .chairs}, composed by [IAPR-TC22](https://iapr-tc22.org/) members.

## Overview of the submission and review process

The review process is the following:

1. Each **author**{: .author} of an accepted paper to main conference **can apply**{: .author} to the Reproducible Research Label by **creating a new issue**{: .author} on the [dedicated Github repository](https://github.com/IAPR-TC22-RRL/ICPR-2026-Reproducible-Research-Label-submissions-test/). This issue must include in particular:
    - an URL of the **Git repository**{: .author} containing the source code used to obtain the results of the paper
    - an indication of the elements of the paper that the code should reproduce
2. The **ICPR Reproducible Research Chairs**{: .chairs} use the same system than the papers ([CMT](https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICPR2026/)) **assign the reviewers to each created issue**{: .chairs}.
3. Each **reviewer**{: .reviewer} attempts to **compile and execute the source code**{: .reviewer} and reproduce the results reported by the authors.
    - Throughout th review process, the reviewer **complete a "review form"**{: .reviewer}
    - At the end, the reviewer **submit the review form**{: .reviewer} on the [CMT](https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICPR2026/) system, along with his assessment of the reproducibility he observed
4. Finally, the ICPR Reproducibility Chairs act as meta-reviewer to **decide the Reproducible Research Label attrbution**{: .chairs}.

Click on the menus on the top of the page to see details on [authors]({{ "authors" | relative_url }}) and [reviewers]({{ "reviewers" | relative_url }}) procedures.
